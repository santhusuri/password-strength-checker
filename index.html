Password Strength Checker

Strong password must meet certain criteria to ensure security.

These criteria may vary depending on the system or organization, but common requirements include:
Password Requirement Description
Minimum length Usually 8-12 characters
Mix of uppercase and lowercase letters A-Z & a-z
At least one number 0123456789
At least one special character Such as !, @, #, etc.
No easily guessable information Like your name, birthdate, or common words
No sequential characters Like "123" or "abc"
No repeating characters Like "aaa" or "111"


It's also a good idea to use a unique password for each account and to change your passwords regularly.
